Welcome! More About Me!
Hello, my name is Lucy Yang, and I will graduate from Buchanan High School in May of 2023. As of right now, I am attending the Center for Advanced Research and Technology to get the hang of programming and exploring its different stems. Currently, I am enrolled in Web Applications and next year I will be entering Game Design. This year in Web Applications, we, as a whole, learned to collaborate and communicate with peers around us in order to complete a task together. One of the major projects we did was a Tourism website. This project highlighted the usage of media inquiries and displayed the history, cultures, holidays, and location of different countries. Another project we are working on is an interactive game story, for this project we are working in groups of 2. An interactive story game basically moves according to the user's choices and can result in different endings. This project is used to highlight the usage of JavaScript and the way it can make a website interactive.
